THIS IS ONE OF OUR STUDENT PROJECT
- BY YEMISI DADA

I carried out an analysis on a sample superstore dataset and here are some insights uncovered. Total sales were $2,297,201 and profit stood at $286,397. The two top-performing regions are West ($725,457.82) and East ($678,781.24), while Consumer topped the segments; Office Supplies and Technology with profits of $56,330.32 and $70,797.81 respectively. From my Insights, below are key takeaways and recommendations; 1. The profits made from Furniture through the Corporate are boosted by bulk orders and premium pricing, while the profits made from Office Supplies are generated from the Consumer market, due to high purchase volumes and frequent sales. 2. Office Supplies utilize discounts as a strategy to boost sales. However, Technology products maintain the highest profits because of minimal discounts and longer product cycles
